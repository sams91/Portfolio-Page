How can I add value to your business?

You may be wondering why you would need a website, especially if your business has been doing well for a long time without one or you use social media. That’s a fair question and it is not my intention to sell someone something they don’t want or need. Here are a few points to consider when deciding whether you or your business could benefit from having a website:

  • It lets people know your business exists and is still in operation.

  • It’s an easy and cost effective way for you to advertise a sale or let people know you are hiring.

  • It gives new and potential customers a way to discover your business.

  • It adds credibility and professionalism to your business.

  • It allows you to easily communicate pertinent information to your customers such as business hours, location, contact information, etc.

  • Customers can book appointments or buy goods or services through your website.

  • You can showcase the goods or services you offer making it more cost effective than paying for advertising.

  • You can save yourself enormous amounts of time by posting the answers to commonly asked questions on your website.

  • A website provides convenience that attracts customers.
